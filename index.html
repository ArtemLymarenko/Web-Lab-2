Advantages of the Fullstack developer profession

Fullstack = Frontend + Backend. This is a universal programmer. He can develop the client and server parts of the application from scratch, so he is able to independently manage projects.

  • 2500$

    The average salary of a middle developer in Ukraine.

  • Frontend + Backend

    The two most in-demand professions in the IT field.

  • 800+ vacancies

    Monthly for Frontend and Backend specialists.

  • Remote work

    75% of vacancies are remote work, 20% are relocation.

  • Interesting projects

    Websites, online stores, mobile apps, games, social networks, media, etc.

  • The best conditions

    Training programs, health insurance, gym, vacations and day offs.

In the Fullstack online course, you will study in the evenings and master the profession of a developer in 10 months. If you want to start your career in IT faster, leave an application and the manager will help you choose the optimal format.
